# Privacy

This Website is collecting anonymous analytics using [Umami](https://umami.is). Umami is open source and fully GDPR compilant and does not use cookies. The analytics page is selfhosted and for transparency and fun you can view it here [here](https://analytics.feuerstein.dev/share/KvVGs6Qr/feuerstein.dev).

This Website may use cookies to provide features of storing settings (light or dark mode) or for my web-apps to function properly. My website will never use cookies to track you, because that would be against my principles. All cookies are technical necessary or in other words: delicious cookies :D
